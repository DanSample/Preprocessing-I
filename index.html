The Love of Creation

From the time I was a little boy I loved to create. Growing up in a tiny beach town in The Great Northwest was a perfect place for my little mind to run wild. From building sandcastles to treeforts I always loved the idea of designing and creating what I could picture in my mind, but wasn't in front of me....yet.

The Love of Learning

Fast forward to today, no longer is it sandcastles and treeforts. I get to build websites and applications from the ground up, and falling in love with learning was the first step. Since the day I started at Lambda School I have been learning and working with the leading technologies in the industry. Evey new language, framework, library is a new adventure that in the end adds tools to my skillset and makes me a better developer. My Goal has always been to be the best that I can be wherever I am and with whatever I'm doing.
